- AlphaFold task: Mutate the Tianjin PET hydrolase (or IsPETase) to increase thermal stability (T50 > 80 °C) and catalytic efficiency (kcat/Km > 10⁵ M⁻¹s⁻¹) in a simulated HTAM environment.
- Timeline: Q2 2026: Enzyme design; Q3: Lab-scale expression and testing; Q4: Integration with BAETA printing.
