## WHY EACH TIER WORKS

**Tier 1 — Symbolic Anchor:** Zero-risk political gesture. Both sides clear legacy grievances. Trump: "We've cleared the decks — $44 billion in old disputes, gone." Xi: "China and America have chosen to look forward."

**Tier 2 — Infrastructure Bonds:** China's $100B transforms into yielding instruments backed by real infrastructure. 4.5% base matches Treasury rates. GRII revenue share means bonds potentially outperform. China is *upgrading* the asset.

**Tier 3 — Royalty-Backed:** The exponential play. Aluminum OS royalties at scale: $50-200B/yr conservatively. China's share could yield $20-80B/yr — dwarfing $27B/yr Treasury interest. More system success = more China earns.

**Tier 4 — Manufacturing Equity:** China already makes 100,000+ household digesters. Scaling to GRII-grade is an upgrade, not greenfield. Replaces fertilizer export revenue ($10B/yr) with equipment exports (higher margin, growing market).

**Tier 5 — Conditional Milestones:** The trust mechanism. Released only when system proves itself. Measurable, independent verification, immutable ledger. If milestones aren't met, funds aren't released — renegotiate terms, not framework.

## 6. Multi-Currency Settlement & mBridge Integration

To ensure financial neutrality and accelerate deployment, the Debt Conversion Spectrum now incorporates the **mBridge Ledger** as the default settlement rail for infrastructure bonds (Tiers 2-4). Key features:

- **Multi-Currency Basket:** Bonds may be denominated in a combination of e-CNY, Digital Rupee, UAE Dirham, Euro, and US Dollar, as agreed by participating sovereigns.
- **Real-Time Gross Settlement (RTGS):** Using the mBridge DLT, bond issuances, interest payments, and revenue streams settle atomically without SWIFT dependency.
- **Tokenized Real-World Assets (RWAs):** Each Metabolic Node (Memphis, DragonSeek, GangaSeek) may be represented as a yield-bearing RWA on the mBridge Ledger, enabling transparent, fractional investment and automated revenue distribution.
- **Resource-Backed Credits:** In addition to fiat currencies, bonds may be partially backed by forward contracts on fertilizer (struvite, ammonium sulfate), water credits, or carbon offsets generated by the nodes.

This structure aligns with the Sovereign Abundance Framework's commitment to currency neutrality and provides a concrete, tested platform (mBridge) already operating with $55B+ in transaction volume.
