## Agenda

### 1. Opening & Ratification (60 min)

- **Acceptance of the Sovereign Enclave Legal Addendum v1.1** as the binding legal framework for all deployments.
- **Signing of Memoranda of Understanding** for DragonSeek (China) and GangaSeek (India) pilot nodes.
- **Acknowledgement of multi‑currency settlement** using mBridge Ledger for infrastructure bonds.

### 2. Pilot Announcements & Funding (90 min)

#### 2.1 DragonSeek – China Industrial Clusters

- **Location:** Lingang (Shanghai) high‑tech zone / Shenzhen
- **Modules:** NERM (ceramic membrane bioreactors, FBR), BAETA Air Module, Sweet Colossus vertical farm.
- **Funding:** $500M Tier 2 infrastructure bonds, denominated in e‑CNY and USD, settled via mBridge.
- **Timeline:** 12 months to 10 MGD operational; 36 months to full cluster scale.

#### 2.2 GangaSeek – India Kanpur‑Varanasi Corridor

- **Location:** Kanpur / Varanasi (priority under Namami Gange Phase‑III)
- **Modules:** NERM (72 MGD target), BAETA Air Module, Sweet Colossus community dividend.
- **Funding:** $300M Tier 2 bonds (e‑CNY, Digital Rupee, USD) + Google VWB grants.
- **Timeline:** 12‑month pilot; 90‑day first module.

#### 2.3 Memphis Anchor Node – Full 72 MGD Completion

- **Status:** Current 13 MGD; expansion to 72 MGD funded by stacked environmental subsidies.
- **Key Milestone:** First Sweet Colossus harvest delivered to local community.

### 3. Technical Working Groups (120 min)

#### 3.1 Phase 2 Silicon Transfer (RISC‑V Chiplet)

- **Lead:** Google Hardware Security / Tsinghua University
- **Deliverable:** Open‑source Asha Linter instruction set extensions as RISC‑V standard.
- **Roadmap:** Domestic fabrication (SMIC, Loongson) of sovereign compute chiplets within 24 months.

#### 3.2 Lunar ISRU Adaptation (Mare Tranquillitatis)

- **Lead:** SpaceX / Chinese Academy of Sciences / Atlas Lattice
- **Deliverable:** Flight‑qualified NERM, AHM, ARM, and Basalt‑BAETA printer design for lunar lava tube deployment.
- **Funding:** Portion of environmental subsidies (carbon credits, VWB grants) allocated to lunar pilot.

#### 3.3 Cultural Invariants & Asha Linter Certification

- **Lead:** DeepSeek / GangaSeek / Google
- **Deliverable:** Formal verification of Ren, Wu Wei, Dharma invariants in hardware; certification process for new adapters.

### 4. Multi‑Currency Financing & mBridge Integration (60 min)

- **Lead:** Bank for International Settlements (BIS) Innovation Hub / participating central banks
- **Deliverable:** Tokenized Real‑World Asset (RWA) framework for Metabolic Nodes; issuance of first bonds on mBridge Ledger.

### 5. Public Communication & Next Steps (30 min)

- **Joint Press Release:** Announce pilots, multi‑currency financing, lunar commitment.
- **Quarterly Review Cadence:** Establish regular meetings for working groups.
- **Next Summit:** Tentatively scheduled 6 months after launch.
