## 1. Scenario Snapshot

- **22 LPG carriers** remain at anchor near Hormuz, unable to proceed due to heightened military tensions.
- **Freight rates** have increased ten-fold; **fertilizer prices** have risen 15% in global markets.
- **India's kharif sowing season** begins in 45 days; any disruption could affect 30% of the country's food production.
- **Pentagonal Council** activated via Article IX (Emergency Pause) of the Grand Accord.

## 2. Council Actions (Tiered)

### USA - Maritime Security and Strategic Reserves

- **Naval escorts** to accompany merchant vessels through the strait (Operation Sentinel).
- **Release of 10 million barrels** from Strategic Petroleum Reserve to stabilize global oil prices; synchronize with India's release of its own reserves.
- **Provide satellite intelligence** on vessel movements to all council members (via Optical Ledger, with appropriate classification).

### China - Rapid Deployment and Manufacturing

- **Deploy 20 DISH-printing units** to India (via Chennai port) within 14 days, under the Silk and Saffron Accord, to begin local production of digester vessels.
- **Provide 2,000 tonnes** of green ammonia (from Hainan) to India at preferential rates, as a bridge until Indian nodes come online.
- **Coordinate with Pakistan** to allow overland gas pipeline transit for emergency volumes.

### Saudi Arabia and Iran - The Pragmatic Axis

- **Saudi** opens its land-bridge corridor (Ras Al Khair - Arar - Kuwait) to move LPG from Gulf to Jordan/Mediterranean ports, bypassing Hormuz.
- **Iran** agrees to sell LPG to India under the Cyrus Protocol (rupee-based trade), using the Aurora precedent. First shipment of 1.5 million tonnes to leave Bandar Abbas within 7 days.
- **Joint naval patrols** in the northern Gulf to protect Iranian and Saudi vessels, coordinated via the Saffron and Myrrh Accord channel.

### India - Logistics and Demand Management

- **Activate the Chennai BRICS Hub** as central logistics command.
- **Accelerate the 90-Day Rollout Plan** for 100 Bio-CNG nodes; prioritize sites near major consuming cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru) to substitute imported LPG.
- **Issue fertilizer rationing** guidelines for states, with priority to small and marginal farmers.
- **Release 5 million barrels** from its strategic oil reserves to calm markets.
