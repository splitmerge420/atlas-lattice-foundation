## I. THE BASELINE: Why India Needs GRII

### Fertilizer Import Dependency

### The Hormuz Vulnerability

As of March 2026:
- 90% of India's LPG imports transit the Strait of Hormuz.
- Fertilizer imports depend on West Asian natural gas (feedstock for urea/ammonia).
- Freight rates risen ten-fold.
- Kharif sowing season approaching - fertilizer availability is food security.

### REAL-WORLD VALIDATION (March 27, 2026)

Crisil Report (this week): Middle East conflict could cut domestic urea and complex fertilizer production by 10-15%.
- ~20% of urea and one-third of complex fertilizers (primarily DAP) imported.
- 60-65% of LNG (urea feedstock) and 75-80% of ammonia from Middle East.
- Fertilizer subsidy bill could rise by Rs 20,000-25,000 crore.
- Inventory (~3 months) cushions immediate blow, but prolonged crisis threatens kharif.

Union Budget 2026-27: Rs 1.71 lakh crore allocated to Department of Fertilizers. Redirecting 10% (Rs 17,000 crore) toward FOM incentives would fully fund IBA's Rs 10,000 crore capital subsidy for Bio-CNG nodes.

Emergency diversification underway: 2.2 million tonnes US LPG locked in for 2026, Argentine supply doubled, first Iranian LPG cargo via Aurora (rupee payment).

### The Waste Problem (Also a Resource)

India's untapped waste streams:
- 1.2-1.5 billion tonnes cattle dung annually (300M+ cattle).
- 92 million tonnes crop residue burned (losing 0.824M tonnes NPK worth Rs 3,850 crore).
- 62 million tonnes municipal solid waste.

### Proven Model: Banas Dairy Bio-CNG Plant

Operating for 6 years in Gujarat:
- 100 tonnes dung/day processing capacity.
- Rs 12 crore annual revenue (Rs 3 lakh/day).
- 6,750 tonnes CO2e reduction annually.
- 400-450 farming families with steady supplementary income (Rs 1/kg dung).

NOW EXPANDING: Adopted by nearly 15 states. Gujarat allocated Rs 60 crore for 10 new plants.

### National Scale

- GOBARdhan: 979 plants across 51.4% of districts.
- SATAT: Targeting 5,000 CBG plants nationally.
- IBA: Proposed Rs 10,000 crore fund for Bio-CNG capital subsidies.
- IGL: 22 partners empaneled (Jan 2026) for 250 TPD CBG over 3-4 years.
